\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
These are convenience macros that are specific to the \doxylink{group__opus__multistream_gae14328330c548dede66c494f51e33707}{opus\+\_\+multistream\+\_\+encoder\+\_\+ctl()} and \doxylink{group__opus__multistream_ga4b3dca8d46e5868cc133f3f6d2b57688}{opus\+\_\+multistream\+\_\+decoder\+\_\+ctl()} interface. 

The CTLs from \doxylink{group__opus__genericctls}{Generic CTLs}, \doxylink{group__opus__encoderctls}{Encoder related CTLs}, and \doxylink{group__opus__decoderctls}{Decoder related CTLs} may be applied to a multistream encoder or decoder as well. In addition, you may retrieve the encoder or decoder state for an specific stream via \doxylink{group__opus__multistream__ctls_ga4e67607eb691c4314a5de90d8c58aff0}{OPUS\+\_\+\+MULTISTREAM\+\_\+\+GET\+\_\+\+ENCODER\+\_\+\+STATE} or \doxylink{group__opus__multistream__ctls_gaf0843831519b4d9b9d73391afef04a53}{OPUS\+\_\+\+MULTISTREAM\+\_\+\+GET\+\_\+\+DECODER\+\_\+\+STATE} and apply CTLs to it individually.
